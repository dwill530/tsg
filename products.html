Gravel

Landscape gravel has many uses and comes in a variety of sizes and colors to suit a property owner's needs.

Pea Gravel

Pea Gravel is great for outdoor landscape projects. This landscaping rock gravel can be used for a dog yard, walkway, driveway and more.

Crushed Rock

Crushed Rock are used as mulches or ground covers to improve the appearance of landscape.

Salt & Pepper

Salt and pepper granite are one of high-end landscapers’ and designers’ favorite types of rock of because of its beautiful appearance.

Drain Rock

Drainage Rock is an all-purpose gravel that can also be used f or other landscaping applications. The natural, gray brick chips are nearly maintenance free.

Ginger Gravel

Ginger Gravel is yellowish gold in color and is a lightweight gravel. It is also significantly water absorbent.

Lodi Gold Gravel

Lodi Gold Gravel is a favorite of landscape designers and contractors as its earth tone colors make it suitable for use in a wide range of designs.

Type II Aggregate Base

Type II Aggregate Base Rock is produced from 100 percent crushed concrete and asphalt concrete products. It is designed to form a base for roads, parking lots, driveways and sidewalks.

Cobble (River Rock)

River Rock Cobbles are blue, grey, and burgundy in color. They can be used as decorative edging for pathways, borders, ponds and landscaping. River rock is desirable for outdoor spaces that need a little more color or depth.

Bark

Landscape bark and hardwood chips are often used around flowerbeds, trees and shrubs as a mulch material.

Deco Bark (Small & Large)

Small Deco Bark is an attractive blend of Douglas Fir & Pine Bark. It can also be used as a general ground cover for a colorful finished look to bare ground.

Walk-On Bar

Walk-On Bark is a small chipped up bark that has a shredded look. With immediate improvement to any landscape, it adds a great look and smell.

Topsoil

Premium Topsoil is the highest grade in the Lake Tahoe area. We only choose to sell soil from the richest, most fertile areas that are available to us.

Certified Organic Compost

Certified Organic Compost is a weed-free soil amendment comprised of decomposed forest products, rice hulls, gypsum, and steer manure.

Blended Topsoil

Blended Topsoil is plan table blend (equal parts organic compost, volcanic soil and screened native soil).

Root Zone Mix

Root Zone Mix is good for top-dressing lawn after aeration (80% Sand/20% Organic Compost) Screened Native Soil.

Paving Stones

Pave your way to the perfect project and turn your landscape into stunning ever-after's with our variety of paving stones.

Border Block

Border Block/Edging Stones add an eye-catching angle of elegance to your gardens and landscaping while ensuring grass, mulch, and other natural delights stay rather than stray.

Turf Stone

The unique design of Turf stone allows rainwater to be gradually filtered back into the soil while stabilizing it to prevent soil erosion.

Mission Pavers

The Mission Paver Stone will transform any backyard or driveway into an Italian veranda.

Classic Pavers

A Mediterranean look and feel are achieved with the Classic Cobble design. Smooth rounded edges, rich colors and variety of installation patterns create an old-world charm.

Universal Pavers

This eye-pleasing interlocking paving stone is the ideal choice for high traffic areas. The unique jig-saw shape can be placed in a variety of patterns.

Retaining Walls

Make a statement and add some dimension to your yard. The designer’s building blocks, retaining walls add visual interest whichever way you decide to incorporate them into your landscaping makeover.

Terraced Walls

Terraced retaining walls and in-wall planters can create more usable space, tame slopes, build raised gardens and will give your yard a more aesthetic look.

Estate Walls

Another great retaining wall system option is our estate wall stones. Natural looking stone that comes in several sizes and shades.

Geowall Pro

Geowall Max

The Geowall Max is for taller gravity and reinforced wall structures.

Bayfield Freestanding Wall

StoneWall II

Retaining Wall Pins
