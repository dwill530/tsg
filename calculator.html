How Much Material Will You Need?

Use our online calculator to figure out roughly how much material you will need. Simply enter your yard dimensions below and we’ll guide you on how much material you will need.

*This calculator will round up in 1/4 cubic yard increments.

How to Use this Construction Materials Calculator

A cubic yard is the volume of material which fits in a space one yard wide by one yard deep by one yard high. Cubic yards are used to measure materials ranging from concrete to mulch for your garden.

  • Coverage Area Length – First you need to carefully measure the length of your project's coverage area. The calculator requires this measurement to be taken in Feet (ft). Double check your measurement, then enter that number.
  • Coverage Area Width – Next, enter the width of your coverage area into the calculator. Just like the length, this number also needs to be in Feet.
  • Coverage Area Depth – The final number the calculator will need, is the depth. However, this number will need to be supplied in Inches (In). Enter this number into the 3rd box and your calculation will then be complete.
