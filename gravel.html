Gravel

Products to Make Your Dream A Reality
  1. Home
  2. Products
  3. Gravel

When you need rock and gravel for landscaping, driveways, pathways, RV pads or any other reason you can rely on Tahoe Sand & Gravel to deliver the right amount for a great price. We carry a vast selection of larger gravel for aesthetic landscape design, down to pea gravel that’s ideal for paver stones, dog runs, and colored gravel water features. At Tahoe Sand & Gravel, we strive to provide our customers with quality aggregates, friendly and knowledgeable services, and reliable deliveries to ensure you have the landscaping gravel and materials you need quickly, affordably, and hassle-free.

Please be advised: Due to variations in color, size and textures in natural products - we cannot guarantee a perfect match. Plus, gravels and masonry stones may vary.
Pea Gravel

Pea Gravel is great for outdoor landscape projects. This landscaping rock gravel can be used for a dog yard, walkway, driveway and more.

Crushed Rock

Crushed Rock are used as mulches or ground covers to improve the appearance of landscape.

Salt & Pepper

Salt and pepper granite are one of high-end landscapers’ and designers’ favorite types of rock of because of its beautiful appearance.

Drain Rock

Drainage Rock is an all-purpose gravel that can also be used f or other landscaping applications. The natural, gray brick chips are nearly maintenance free.

Ginger Gravel

Ginger Gravel is yellowish gold in color and is a lightweight gravel. It is also significantly water absorbent.

Lodi Gold Gravel

Lodi Gold Gravel is a favorite of landscape designers and contractors as its earth tone colors make it suitable for use in a wide range of designs.

Type II Aggregate Base

Type II Aggregate Base Rock is produced from 100 percent crushed concrete and asphalt concrete products. It is designed to form a base for roads, parking lots, driveways and sidewalks.

Cobble (River Rock)

River Rock Cobbles are blue, grey, and burgundy in color. They can be used as decorative edging for pathways, borders, ponds and landscaping. River rock is desirable for outdoor spaces that need a little more color or depth.
